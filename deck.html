Why voice? Because donors don't open apps in India.

  • 540M Indians live in "blood deserts" — no blood bank within 30km
  • Donors are blue-collar workers, students — not on apps
  • Phone call in mother tongue gets 12× the response of apps
  • Ringg: multilingual, parallel, structured — at ₹6/call

This can never be an app.

Donors don't download apps "just in case." Emergencies are unpredictable. You need someone who donated 2 years ago to pick up today. An app notification gets ignored. A phone call in Kannada at the right time — that gets answered.

The agent doesn't pitch. It listens.

"Namaste, aap O+ blood donate kar sakte hain kya? Hospital 3km door hai. Kya aap aa sakte hain?"
